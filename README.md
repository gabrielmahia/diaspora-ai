## Who this is for

3+ million Kenyans abroad. Most are navigating questions like:

- *"How much tax do I owe on money I send home?"*
- *"Can I invest in a plot in Kenya from the USA?"*
- *"My parents need hospital care — how do I pay from here?"*
- *"I want to apply for dual citizenship — what's the process?"*
- *"I'm returning to Kenya next year — what do I need to prepare?"*

No tool answers these. DiasporaAI does.

## What it covers

| Topic | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 💸 **Remittances** | True cost comparison, FBAR reporting, gift tax thresholds |

| 🏥 **Remote family care** | NHIF from abroad, MPESA for medical bills, hospital selection |

| ✈️ **Return planning** | KRA PIN, account reactivation, school systems, job market |

## Important disclaimer

DiasporaAI provides **general information**, not legal, tax, or financial advice. Always consult a licensed CPA, immigration attorney, or financial advisor for your specific situation.
